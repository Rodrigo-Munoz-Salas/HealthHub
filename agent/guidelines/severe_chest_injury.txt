Severe Chest Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not move person unless necessary
3. Check for signs of breathing difficulty
4. Monitor vital signs
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Difficulty breathing
- Chest pain with breathing
- Rapid or shallow breathing
- Blue lips or fingernails
- Signs of shock
- Visible chest deformity

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Chest injuries can be life-threatening
- Risk of collapsed lung or heart damage
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Wear protective equipment
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
