Severe Anxiety Attack Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Help person find quiet, safe space
2. Encourage slow, deep breathing
3. Stay calm and reassuring
4. Call 911 if severe or with other symptoms
5. Monitor for signs of medical emergency

WARNING SIGNS:
- Chest pain with anxiety
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe dizziness or fainting
- Signs of heart attack
- Severe panic that doesn't improve
- Thoughts of self-harm

EMERGENCY LEVEL: MODERATE
- Severe anxiety can mimic serious medical conditions
- May indicate underlying medical problem
- Risk of self-harm in severe cases
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Practice stress management techniques
- Regular exercise
- Adequate sleep
- Avoid excessive caffeine
- Seek professional help if needed
- Regular medical checkups
