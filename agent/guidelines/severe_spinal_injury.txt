Severe Spinal Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not move person
3. Support head and neck
4. Monitor breathing and consciousness
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe back or neck pain
- Loss of feeling or movement
- Difficulty breathing
- Loss of bladder or bowel control
- Signs of shock
- Loss of consciousness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Spinal injuries can cause paralysis
- Risk of permanent damage
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Wear protective equipment
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
