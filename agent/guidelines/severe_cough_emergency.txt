Severe Cough Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Help person sit upright
2. Monitor breathing pattern
3. Check for signs of choking
4. Call 911 if severe breathing difficulty
5. Monitor for signs of respiratory distress

WARNING SIGNS:
- Coughing up blood
- Severe breathing difficulty
- Blue lips or fingernails
- High fever with cough
- Chest pain with cough
- Sudden onset of severe cough

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Severe cough can indicate serious respiratory condition
- Blood in cough may indicate serious illness
- Risk of respiratory failure
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Avoid smoking and secondhand smoke
- Stay hydrated
- Use humidifier in dry environments
- Avoid irritants and allergens
- Regular exercise
- Regular medical checkups
