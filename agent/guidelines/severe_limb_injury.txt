Severe Limb Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not move injured limb
3. Check for signs of circulation
4. Monitor for signs of shock
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe pain in limb
- Visible deformity
- Loss of feeling or movement
- Signs of shock
- Heavy bleeding
- Loss of consciousness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Limb injuries can cause permanent damage
- Risk of shock
- May require immediate treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Wear protective equipment
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
