Severe Neck Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not move person's head or neck
3. Support head in current position
4. Monitor breathing and consciousness
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe neck pain
- Difficulty breathing
- Weakness or numbness in arms/legs
- Loss of consciousness
- Signs of shock
- Inability to move neck

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Neck injuries can cause paralysis
- Risk of spinal cord damage
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Wear protective equipment
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
