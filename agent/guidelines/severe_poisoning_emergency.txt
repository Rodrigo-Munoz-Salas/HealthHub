Severe Poisoning Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Call poison control center
3. Do not induce vomiting unless instructed
4. Monitor breathing and consciousness
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Loss of consciousness
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe nausea or vomiting
- Signs of shock
- Seizures
- Severe abdominal pain

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Poisoning can be life-threatening
- Risk of death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Keep poisons out of reach
- Use child safety locks
- Read labels carefully
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
