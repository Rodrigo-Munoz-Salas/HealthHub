Severe Joint Pain Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Help person find comfortable position
2. Check for signs of joint injury
3. Apply ice if appropriate
4. Call 911 if severe or with other symptoms
5. Monitor for signs of infection

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe joint pain with swelling
- Joint pain with fever
- Joint pain with redness and warmth
- Sudden severe joint pain
- Joint pain with inability to move joint
- Joint pain after injury

EMERGENCY LEVEL: MODERATE
- Severe joint pain may indicate serious condition
- Joint infection requires immediate treatment
- Risk of permanent joint damage
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Maintain healthy weight
- Exercise regularly with proper form
- Use proper protective equipment
- Avoid repetitive stress on joints
- Regular medical checkups
- Manage underlying conditions
