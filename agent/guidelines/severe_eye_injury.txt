Severe Eye Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not rub or touch the eye
3. Do not remove embedded objects
4. Cover eye with clean cloth
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe eye pain
- Vision loss or changes
- Blood in the eye
- Embedded object in eye
- Chemical exposure to eye
- Inability to open eye

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Eye injuries can cause permanent vision loss
- Risk of blindness
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Wear protective eyewear
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
