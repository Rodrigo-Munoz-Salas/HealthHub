Severe Snake Bite Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Keep person calm and still
3. Remove jewelry from affected area
4. Do not cut or suck wound
5. Monitor vital signs

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe pain at bite site
- Swelling and discoloration
- Difficulty breathing
- Signs of shock
- Loss of consciousness
- Signs of allergic reaction

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Snake bites can be life-threatening
- Risk of death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Avoid areas with snakes
- Wear protective clothing
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Stay on marked trails
