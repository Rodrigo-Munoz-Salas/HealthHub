Severe Insect Sting Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Remove stinger if present
3. Check for signs of allergic reaction
4. Monitor breathing and pulse
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Difficulty breathing
- Swelling of face or throat
- Signs of shock
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe pain
- Signs of allergic reaction

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Insect stings can cause severe allergic reactions
- Risk of anaphylaxis
- May require immediate treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Avoid areas with insects
- Use insect repellent
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Carry epinephrine if allergic
