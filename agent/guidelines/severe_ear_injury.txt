Severe Ear Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not insert anything in ear
3. Check for signs of hearing loss
4. Monitor for signs of head injury
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe ear pain
- Hearing loss
- Blood or fluid from ear
- Dizziness or balance problems
- Signs of head injury
- Loss of consciousness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Ear injuries can cause permanent hearing loss
- Risk of balance problems
- May indicate head injury
- Seek medical attention immediately

PREVENTION:
- Wear protective equipment
- Avoid loud noises
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
