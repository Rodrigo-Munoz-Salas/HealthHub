Severe Animal Bite Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Control bleeding with pressure
3. Clean wound if possible
4. Monitor for signs of infection
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe bleeding
- Signs of shock
- Loss of consciousness
- Signs of infection
- Severe pain
- Difficulty breathing

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Animal bites can cause serious infections
- Risk of rabies
- May require immediate treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Avoid contact with wild animals
- Vaccinate pets
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Supervise children around animals
