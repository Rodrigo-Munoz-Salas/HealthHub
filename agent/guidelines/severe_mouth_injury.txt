Severe Mouth Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not try to remove embedded objects
3. Apply gentle pressure to stop bleeding
4. Monitor for signs of choking
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe mouth pain
- Heavy bleeding that won't stop
- Difficulty breathing or swallowing
- Embedded objects in mouth
- Signs of head injury
- Loss of consciousness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Mouth injuries can affect breathing
- Risk of choking
- May require immediate treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Wear protective equipment
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
