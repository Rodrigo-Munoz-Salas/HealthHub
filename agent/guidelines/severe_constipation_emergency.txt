Severe Constipation Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Assess severity and duration
2. Check for signs of bowel obstruction
3. Monitor for severe pain
4. Call 911 if severe or with other symptoms
5. Help person stay hydrated

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe abdominal pain
- Vomiting with constipation
- No bowel movement for more than 7 days
- Severe bloating or distension
- Blood in stool
- Signs of dehydration

EMERGENCY LEVEL: MODERATE
- Severe constipation may indicate bowel obstruction
- Can cause serious complications
- Risk of perforation
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Eat high-fiber diet
- Stay hydrated
- Exercise regularly
- Establish regular bathroom routine
- Regular medical checkups
- Avoid excessive use of laxatives
