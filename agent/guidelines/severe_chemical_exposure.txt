Severe Chemical Exposure Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Remove person from exposure
3. Remove contaminated clothing
4. Rinse affected areas with water
5. Monitor vital signs

WARNING SIGNS:
- Difficulty breathing
- Signs of shock
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe burns
- Signs of poisoning
- Severe pain

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Chemical exposure can be life-threatening
- Risk of death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use protective equipment
- Follow safety procedures
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Store chemicals safely
