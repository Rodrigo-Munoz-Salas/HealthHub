Severe Medication Reaction Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Check for signs of allergic reaction
3. Do not give more medication
4. Monitor breathing and pulse
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Difficulty breathing
- Swelling of face or throat
- Signs of shock
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe rash
- Signs of allergic reaction

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Medication reactions can be life-threatening
- Risk of anaphylaxis
- May require immediate treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Take medications as prescribed
- Report allergies to healthcare providers
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Carry medical alert bracelet
