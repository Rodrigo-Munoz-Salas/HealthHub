Severe Depression Crisis Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Take all threats seriously
2. Stay with person if suicidal
3. Remove any means of self-harm
4. Call 911 or crisis hotline immediately
5. Do not leave person alone

WARNING SIGNS:
- Talking about suicide or death
- Giving away possessions
- Sudden mood changes
- Withdrawing from others
- Increased substance use
- Expressing hopelessness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Suicidal thoughts are a medical emergency
- Risk of self-harm is immediate
- Time is critical
- Call 911 or crisis hotline immediately

PREVENTION:
- Seek professional mental health care
- Build support network
- Practice self-care
- Regular medical checkups
- Learn coping strategies
- Know crisis resources
