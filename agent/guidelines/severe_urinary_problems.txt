Severe Urinary Problems Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Assess symptoms and severity
2. Check for signs of infection
3. Monitor vital signs
4. Call 911 if severe or with other symptoms
5. Help person stay hydrated

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe pain during urination
- Blood in urine
- Inability to urinate
- Severe abdominal or back pain
- High fever with urinary symptoms
- Signs of dehydration

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Severe urinary problems may indicate serious condition
- Kidney stones or infection can be severe
- Risk of kidney damage
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Stay hydrated
- Practice good hygiene
- Urinate regularly
- Avoid holding urine for long periods
- Regular medical checkups
- Manage underlying conditions
