Severe Drug Overdose Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Check for breathing and pulse
3. Do not induce vomiting
4. Monitor vital signs
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Loss of consciousness
- Difficulty breathing
- Slow or irregular pulse
- Signs of shock
- Seizures
- Severe confusion

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Drug overdose can be life-threatening
- Risk of death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Take medications as prescribed
- Avoid mixing drugs and alcohol
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Seek help for substance abuse
