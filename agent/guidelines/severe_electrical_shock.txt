Severe Electrical Shock Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not touch person until power is off
3. Turn off power source if safe
4. Check for breathing and pulse
5. Monitor vital signs

WARNING SIGNS:
- Loss of consciousness
- Difficulty breathing
- Irregular pulse
- Signs of shock
- Burns at entry/exit points
- Severe muscle contractions

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Electrical shock can be life-threatening
- Risk of death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use electrical safety devices
- Avoid contact with power lines
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Use proper electrical equipment
