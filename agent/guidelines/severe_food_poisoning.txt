Severe Food Poisoning Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not induce vomiting
3. Monitor for signs of dehydration
4. Keep person calm and still
5. Monitor vital signs

WARNING SIGNS:
- Loss of consciousness
- Difficulty breathing
- Signs of shock
- Severe dehydration
- Blood in vomit or stool
- High fever

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Food poisoning can be life-threatening
- Risk of severe dehydration
- May require immediate treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Practice good food hygiene
- Cook food thoroughly
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Avoid contaminated food
