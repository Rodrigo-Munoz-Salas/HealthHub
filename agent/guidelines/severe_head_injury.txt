Severe Head Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not move person unless necessary
3. Check for signs of consciousness
4. Monitor breathing and pulse
5. Keep person still and calm

WARNING SIGNS:
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe headache
- Nausea or vomiting
- Confusion or disorientation
- Unequal pupil size
- Clear fluid from nose or ears

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Head injuries can be life-threatening
- Risk of brain damage
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Wear helmets during activities
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
