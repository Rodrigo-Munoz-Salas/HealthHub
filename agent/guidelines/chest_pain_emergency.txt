Chest Pain Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately if experiencing chest pain
2. Have the person sit down and rest
3. Loosen any tight clothing
4. Monitor breathing and consciousness
5. Do not give food or water

WARNING SIGNS:
- Shortness of breath
- Nausea or vomiting
- Sweating
- Pain radiating to arm, jaw, or back
- Dizziness or fainting
- Pressure or squeezing sensation in chest

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Any chest pain should be treated as a potential heart attack
- Time is critical - call 911 immediately
- Do not delay seeking medical attention

PREVENTION:
- Maintain healthy blood pressure
- Control cholesterol levels
- Exercise regularly
- Avoid smoking
- Manage stress levels
- Regular medical checkups
