Severe Abdominal Injury Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Do not give food or water
3. Check for signs of internal bleeding
4. Monitor vital signs
5. Keep person calm and still

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe abdominal pain
- Signs of shock (pale, cold, rapid pulse)
- Abdominal swelling or distension
- Blood in vomit or stool
- Difficulty breathing
- Loss of consciousness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Abdominal injuries can be life-threatening
- Risk of internal bleeding
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use seat belts in vehicles
- Wear protective equipment
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Maintain good physical condition
- Know first aid techniques
