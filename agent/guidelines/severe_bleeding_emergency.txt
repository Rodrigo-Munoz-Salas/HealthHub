Severe Bleeding Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Apply direct pressure to wound
3. Elevate injured area if possible
4. Do not remove embedded objects
5. Monitor for signs of shock

WARNING SIGNS:
- Blood spurting from wound
- Bleeding that won't stop with pressure
- Large amount of blood loss
- Signs of shock (pale, cold, rapid pulse)
- Bleeding from head, neck, or chest
- Loss of consciousness

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Severe bleeding can be life-threatening
- Risk of shock and death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Use proper safety equipment
- Avoid dangerous activities
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency supplies available
- Avoid sharp objects when possible
