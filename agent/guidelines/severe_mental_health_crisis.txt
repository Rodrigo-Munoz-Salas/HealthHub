Severe Mental Health Crisis Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Stay with person if safe
3. Remove any means of self-harm
4. Keep person calm and still
5. Monitor vital signs

WARNING SIGNS:
- Threats of self-harm
- Signs of psychosis
- Severe agitation
- Loss of reality
- Signs of overdose
- Severe confusion

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Mental health crises can be life-threatening
- Risk of self-harm
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Seek professional help
- Take medications as prescribed
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Build support network
