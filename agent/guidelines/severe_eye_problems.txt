Severe Eye Problems Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Assess vision changes
2. Check for signs of eye injury
3. Do not rub or touch affected eye
4. Call 911 if severe or sudden vision loss
5. Cover eye if injury suspected

WARNING SIGNS:
- Sudden vision loss
- Severe eye pain
- Eye injury with foreign object
- Severe headache with vision changes
- Double vision
- Flashing lights or floaters

EMERGENCY LEVEL: HIGH
- Sudden vision loss requires immediate treatment
- Eye injury can cause permanent damage
- Risk of blindness
- Seek medical attention immediately

PREVENTION:
- Wear protective eyewear
- Regular eye exams
- Avoid eye strain
- Protect eyes from UV light
- Regular medical checkups
- Manage underlying conditions
