Severe Sore Throat Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Check for signs of airway obstruction
2. Monitor breathing pattern
3. Check for signs of infection
4. Call 911 if severe breathing difficulty
5. Help person stay hydrated

WARNING SIGNS:
- Severe difficulty swallowing
- Breathing difficulty
- High fever with sore throat
- Swollen neck or lymph nodes
- Severe pain that prevents eating/drinking
- Signs of dehydration

EMERGENCY LEVEL: MODERATE
- Severe sore throat can indicate serious infection
- Risk of airway obstruction
- May require immediate medical treatment
- Seek medical attention if severe

PREVENTION:
- Practice good hand hygiene
- Avoid close contact with sick people
- Stay hydrated
- Get adequate rest
- Regular medical checkups
- Avoid irritants like smoke
