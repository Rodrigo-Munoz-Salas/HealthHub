Severe Smoke Inhalation Emergency Guidelines

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Call 911 immediately
2. Remove person from smoke
3. Check for breathing and pulse
4. Begin CPR if needed
5. Monitor vital signs

WARNING SIGNS:
- Loss of consciousness
- Difficulty breathing
- No pulse
- Signs of shock
- Blue lips or fingernails
- Severe confusion

EMERGENCY LEVEL: CRITICAL
- Smoke inhalation can be life-threatening
- Risk of death
- Time is critical
- Call 911 immediately

PREVENTION:
- Install smoke detectors
- Have fire escape plan
- Regular medical checkups
- Know first aid techniques
- Keep emergency numbers available
- Practice fire safety
